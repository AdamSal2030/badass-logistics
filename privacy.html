Privacy Policy

Short version: we collect your contact info so we can quote your load, we don't sell it, and we don't track you around the internet.

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you submit our quote form, we receive the information you type in: your name, company, email, phone number, and the details of your shipment. Our form is processed by Formspree, which delivers your submission to our inbox; Formspree's own privacy policy covers their handling. If you call or email us directly, we have whatever you share with us.

How we use it

To respond to your quote request, plan and price your move, and follow up about your job. That's it. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their marketing.

What we don't do

This site sets no advertising cookies and runs no third-party analytics or tracking scripts. Pages load fonts from Google Fonts and map embeds from Google Maps; those services may receive standard technical data (like your IP address) when your browser loads them, per Google's privacy policy.

Data retention & your choices

Quote requests stay in our inbox as part of normal business records. If you want your information corrected or deleted, email us and we'll take care of it.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: info@badasslogistics.com or (307) 284-1332.
