Location FAQs

Do you have a physical office in every city you list?
No. Badass Logistics is a service-area company — we dispatch crews, carriers, and rigging equipment to your site from a nationwide network, so we cover a metro without needing a storefront there. Our base of operations is in Kalispell, Montana.
Do you really cover all 50 states?
Yes. We coordinate heavy haul, rigging, machinery moving, and freight across all 50 U.S. states, routing the nearest qualified crew and the right equipment to your pickup and delivery points.
How fast can you get to my location?
It depends on the job and the lane, but because we run a nationwide carrier and rigging network rather than a single yard, we can usually mobilize the right equipment quickly. Send your location, dates, and load details for a firm timeline.
Do you charge more for service in remote areas?
Location affects the quote — repositioning specialized equipment and route access both factor in — but there is no flat surcharge. Send the pickup and delivery points and we'll price the actual move.

Need service in your area?

Tell us where and what. We'll route the nearest crew.
