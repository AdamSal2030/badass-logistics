One crew for the whole move

Most logistics problems don't happen during the work — they happen in the gaps between companies. The rigger blames the carrier, the carrier blames the broker, and you're stuck in the middle making phone calls. We started Badass Logistics in 2022 to kill those gaps — one company that owns the whole move instead of four vendors pointing at each other.

By keeping industrial rigging, heavy haul transportation, truck dispatching, and freight moving all under one roof, we own the entire move. One team plans it, one team executes it, and one team answers the phone when you call.

How we work

We treat every job like an engineering problem first — dimensions, weights, clearances, routes, and risks mapped before a wheel turns — then we execute it like a road crew. That's how oversized, overweight, and "nobody-else-will-touch-it" loads get moved on time.
