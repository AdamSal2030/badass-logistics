Trailer board FAQ

What does "release requirements" mean?
Before a trailer leaves our yard or a partner location, we confirm a few things with you — who's hauling it, insurance, timing, and the paperwork for the move. Email trailermoves@badasslogistics.com with the trailer you want and we'll send over exactly what's needed. It's quick.
How current is this board?
It's live — the board updates the moment our trailer desk posts or removes a unit. If a trailer shows here, it was available when the page loaded.
What kind of moves are these?
Mostly power-only and tow-away moves: you bring the tractor, hook our trailer, and deliver it on the listed lane. Lane details, timing, and pay are confirmed by email before you roll.
Can I get on a list for future moves?
Yes — email trailermoves@badasslogistics.com with your equipment, home base, and preferred lanes and we'll reach out when something matches.
