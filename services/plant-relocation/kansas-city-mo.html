Plant & factory relocation in Kansas City, MO

A plant move isn't one big lift — it's dozens of moves in the right order: disconnect, teardown, sequenced transport, reinstall, recommission. Get the sequence wrong and the new floor sits half-built while production bleeds. Across Kansas City's rail & distribution crossroads sector, Badass Logistics owns the whole project with one accountable crew.

We map the path of travel and floor loadings at both sites, tear down and label, haul oversized machines on permitted routes, and rig and set every machine back to spec in the new layout. Single line, full facility, or a multi-site consolidation — one plan, one team, one schedule.

Routes & permits across the Kansas City metro

Moves around Kansas City run the I-70, I-44, I-35 and I-29 corridors that knit the metro's industrial belt together. A load that's legal-dimension just gets hauled; anything over height, width, or weight has to be legal on every mile.
