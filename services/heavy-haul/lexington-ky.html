What trailer will my Lexington load ship on?
It depends on dimensions and weight: step-deck for tall freight, RGN or lowboy for tall/heavy equipment that rolls on, double-drop for extreme height, and multi-axle for superloads. We match the trailer to the load before we quote it.

Yes — we handle Kentucky DOT oversize permitting, plan a legal route on I-65, I-64, I-75 and I-71, and arrange pilot cars and police escorts where the state requires them.
