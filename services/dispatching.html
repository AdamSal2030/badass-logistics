Dispatch built for drivers who'd rather drive

Hunting load boards, haggling rates, and chasing rate cons eats hours you don't have. Our dispatchers do it for you — finding profitable freight that fits your equipment and lanes, locking in strong rates, and handling the booking, BOLs, and broker back-and-forth.

Whether you run one truck or a fleet, we treat your authority like it's our own: keep you loaded, keep your miles paid, and keep you off the phone. And you approve every load before we book it — no forced dispatch, no pressure to run lanes that do not work for your truck.

questions

Dispatch FAQ

What does a dispatch service actually do?
We find loads, negotiate rates, handle booking and paperwork, and plan routes — so you stay focused on driving and keep your truck earning.
Do you work with single owner-operators?
Absolutely — from one truck to a full fleet. We match freight to your equipment, lanes, and rate goals.
How do fees work?
A percentage of the load or a flat weekly rate — lower for dry van, higher for flatbed and specialized freight. You get the exact number upfront before you commit, and no hidden admin fees stacked on top.
What equipment types do you dispatch?
Dry van, reefer, flatbed, step deck, and heavy/oversized — including coordination with our heavy haul team when loads get big.
