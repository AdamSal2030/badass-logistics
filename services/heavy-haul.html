questions

Heavy haul FAQ

What counts as an oversized load?
Generally anything over about 8'6" wide, 13'6" tall, 53' long, or 80,000 lbs gross — limits vary by state. Past those, you need permits and specialized equipment.
Which trailer will my load need?
It depends on height and weight. Tall, heavy machinery usually rides an RGN/lowboy; lighter oversized freight often goes on a step deck or flatbed. Try our trailer selector to see which trailer fits your load and which permits apply — or just send dimensions and we'll spec it for you.
Do you handle the permits?
Yes — we manage oversize/overweight permits, route surveys, and pilot-car coordination so the move is fully legal.
