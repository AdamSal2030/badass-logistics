Heavy haul & rigging across North Carolina

Badass Logistics runs rigging, heavy haul, dispatch, and freight moving throughout North Carolina, with local coverage built around Charlotte. The state's freight moves on the I-40, I-85, I-95 and I-77 corridors, and our crews move with it — machinery, oversize loads, plant relocations, and everyday FTL and LTL freight in the energy & manufacturing market.
