Heavy haul & rigging across Missouri

Badass Logistics runs rigging, heavy haul, dispatch, and freight moving throughout Missouri, with local coverage built around Kansas City and St. Louis. The state's freight moves on the I-70, I-44, I-35 and I-29 corridors, and our crews move with it — machinery, oversize loads, plant relocations, and everyday FTL and LTL freight in the rail & distribution, manufacturing & river markets.

Every oversize or overweight move through Missouri needs to be legal on every mile: state permits, routing around restricted bridges, travel-time windows, and escorts where dimensions require them.
