Heavy haul & rigging across Pennsylvania

Badass Logistics runs rigging, heavy haul, dispatch, and freight moving throughout Pennsylvania, with local coverage built around Philadelphia and Pittsburgh. The state's freight moves on the I-76, I-80, I-81 and I-95 corridors, and our crews move with it — machinery, oversize loads, plant relocations, and everyday FTL and LTL freight in the port & manufacturing, steel & energy markets.

Every oversize or overweight move through Pennsylvania needs to be legal on every mile: state permits, routing around restricted bridges, travel-time windows, and escorts where dimensions require them.
