Heavy haul & rigging across Texas

Badass Logistics runs rigging, heavy haul, dispatch, and freight moving throughout Texas, with local coverage built around Houston, Dallas, San Antonio and Midland. The state's freight moves on the I-10, I-20, I-35 and I-45 corridors, and our crews move with it — machinery, oversize loads, plant relocations, and everyday FTL and LTL freight in the energy & petrochemical, manufacturing & distribution, manufacturing & energy, Permian Basin oilfield markets.

Every oversize or overweight move through Texas needs to be legal on every mile: state permits, routing around restricted bridges, travel-time windows, and escorts where dimensions require them.
